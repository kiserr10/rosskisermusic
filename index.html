Ross Kiser Music

About:

Ross Kiser is a Deep/Tech-House producer and Dj based out of Denver, Colorado. Originally from Boston, Ross began producing house music in college and has carried the art to new heights and across genres over the last couple of years. Known for being one half of the acclaimed dance music duo Need & Necessity, Ross has recently branched out into a solo career to focus on a new sound and style. Ross has always had a love for the deeper and more underground side of dance music and this new direction showcases exactly that!
